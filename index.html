About Me

Hi there! My name is Handong Zhao, 赵汉东 in Chinese. I am a fourth year Ph.D. candidate at Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering, Northeastern University, advised by Prof. Prof. Yun (Raymond) Fu. My research interests include machine learning and computer vision. Before coming to U.S., I received both my master's and bachelor's degrees in Tianjin University, China in 2013 and 2010. I had a great internship experience at IBM T.J Watson Research Center, working closely with Quanfu Fan and Dan Gutfreund in the summer of 2016.
